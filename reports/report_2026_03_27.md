### 3. The Analyst's Confluence Algorithm Has a Structural Mismatch

The 6-signal base algorithm is designed for reversal trades:
- `rsi_favorable` for SHORT requires RSI 60–70 → **always 0 during a selloff** (RSI 20–40)
- `price_at_bollinger` for SHORT requires price ≥ upper band → **always 0** during downtrends
- `rsi_favorable` for LONG requires RSI 30–40 → ironically, this fires during the selloff for LONG, not SHORT

This caps the algorithm at 4/6 max for trend-following entries, requiring 3+ discretionary points to reach 7. The algorithm is structurally biased against the most common trade type (trend continuation).

Of 7 confluence-6 rejections:
- 2 correctly rejected (DOGEUSDT late, BNBUSDT would have bounced)
- 2 debatable (SOLUSDT and BTCUSDT at 10:37)
- 1 wrong rejection (TAOUSDT LONG missed a +17.7% squeeze)
- 2 insufficient data (late-session, no time to trade)

Lowering the threshold to 6 would have allowed: the TAOUSDT LONG (+17.7% winner), but also the BNBUSDT SHORT (loser), BTCUSDT SHORT (loser on bounce), and late-session entries with no edge. **Net: lowering to 6 is not recommended.**

## Recommendations

### P1 — Fix Trader R:R Target Selection

**Problem**: The Trader used immediate Bollinger bands as profit targets ($0.8949 for SUI) instead of structural support levels ($0.80). This led to a calculated R:R of 0.5:1, when the real R:R was ~2:1.

**Fix**: Inject the Analyst's `key_levels` (support/resistance) into the Trader context as explicit target candidates. The Trader should evaluate R:R against the nearest structural level, not just the Bollinger band.

### P2 — Add Trend-Following RSI Mode to Confluence

**Problem**: `rsi_favorable` for SHORT requires RSI 60–70 (overbought reversal). During a trend-following entry, RSI < 50 for SHORT should be confirmation, not a counter-signal.

**Fix**: When `ema_alignment` is True (EMA20 < EMA50 < EMA200), switch to "trend mode" where RSI 30–50 counts as favorable for SHORT. Keep the current 60–70 mode for reversal plays.

**Impact**: Would add 1 point to most trend-following setups, moving several confluence-5 decisions to 6.

### P3 — Add "Walks the Band" to Bollinger Confluence

**Problem**: `price_at_bollinger` for SHORT requires price ≥ upper band. During strong downtrends, price rides the lower band ("walks the bands"). This is a trend-strength signal, but currently scores 0.

**Fix**: When `ema_alignment` is True and price ≤ lower Bollinger band, count `price_at_bollinger` as True for SHORT. Combined with P2, this brings trend-following algorithmic base from 2/6 to 4/6.

### P4 — Reduce Trader Latency

**Problem**: 16–36s response times on 1m timeframe make decisions stale.

**Options**:
- Switch Trader to Sonnet (5–8s vs 16–36s). The Trader reasoning quality was excellent but the time cost is too high for the benefit.
- Pre-compute R:R and key levels in the Spine, giving the Trader a pre-baked "entry plan" to accept/reject.
- Add a 10s timeout with auto-WAIT.

### P5 — Reduce Scout Latency

**Problem**: Scout p50=5.8s (target <2s) bottlenecks the pipeline.

**Options**:
- Verify OpenRouter is using cheapest/fastest model
- Simplify Scout prompt to pure classification (remove detailed reasoning requirement)
- Add 8s timeout with auto-SKIP fallback

### P6 — Add Volume Spike as Standalone Confluence Signal

**Problem**: TAOUSDT's 3.4x volume spike was a strong accumulation signal that the confluence algorithm had no way to capture. The 6 binary signals don't include "extreme volume" as a standalone.

**Fix**: Add a 7th signal: `volume_spike` = True when volume_ratio > 2.5x. This would have added 1 point to TAOUSDT LONG, potentially pushing it to 7.

### P7 — Investigate SUIUSDT VolatilityGate Calibration

**Problem**: SUIUSDT was blocked by VolatilityGate at 10:27 and 10:32 (before the main selloff) due to LOW volatility regime. Then it was blocked during EXTREME at 10:38–10:42. By the time volatility returned to HIGH/NORMAL, the optimal entry had passed.

**Fix**: Consider reducing the VolatilityGate zscore threshold for LOW from −1.0 to −1.5 to allow borderline symbols through, or adding a "warming up" detection that lets through symbols whose regime is transitioning from LOW to NORMAL.
